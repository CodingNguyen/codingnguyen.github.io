About me:


Hello! I am a Senior Computer Engineering student minoring in Math at Texas A&M University. I originally went into college intending to become a doctor, so I began my time at A&M studying premed Biology. However, I soon realized my true interests were in electronics.

I enjoy coming up with creative design solutions for problems, and I have a passion for coding, making, and tinkering. I've accumulated about five Raspberry Pis so far for a variety of personal projects. Outside of engineering, I enjoy illustrating to express my creative side and playing soccer for a team-oriented outlet.

I believe I'd make a great asset to any team. I don't know where I'll end up, but I know I would like to make a difference and maybe learn a thing or two along the way.
